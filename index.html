Fix originally reported here by Dam.

Depending on your system, you may encounter one or both of the issues below - following the instructions should get your Steam back to normal (along with Town of Salem) within just a few minutes.

Issues and how to respond to them

The application "Steam" can't be opened -600

Restart your computer. Upon reinstall, Steam will have to re-update.

Failed to load steamui.dylib

Re-install Steam - drag /Applications/Steam.app to trash (optionally empty trash to save space), and re-download Steam from steampowered.com (or if you use Homebrew Cask: brew install steam --force). Your games will not need to be reinstalled.

I just give up on the app - is there another way?

You can run the fix manually by opening a Terminal session (press the magnifying glass in the top-right and search Terminal), and running the following:

eval `curl https://raw.githubusercontent.com/iggyvolz/ToSFixer/master/ToSFixer`

Or, if you want to see the script as you're doing it:

cd ~/Library/Application\ Support/Steam/steamapps/common/Town\ of\ Salem/TownOfSalem.app/Contents/Frameworks/Adobe\ AIR.framework
cd Versions
[ -f Current ] && rm Current
ln -s 1.0/ Current
cd ..
[ -f Resources ] && rm Resources
[ -f Adobe\ AIR ] && rm Adobe\ AIR
ln -s Versions/Current/Resources .
ln -s Versions/Current/Adobe\ AIR .
